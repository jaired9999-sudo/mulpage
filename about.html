Est. 2018 · London

BUILT ON Conviction

VÖRN was founded in 2018 by Karan Oberoi, a London-based designer who had grown frustrated by the noise of fashion — its cycles, its compromises, its indifference to the man who simply wants to dress well.

The name VÖRN means nothing — it was chosen precisely because it resists easy categorisation. Like the clothes, it exists on its own terms.

What We Believe

OUR Values

01
Heritage Over Hype
We work with mills that have been producing exceptional cloth for over a century. Their knowledge is irreplaceable. Their standards are non-negotiable.
02
Less, Made Better
We make 120 pieces per season. That's it. Each one is considered, constructed to an exacting standard, and designed to be worn for decades — not months.
03
Radical Transparency
Our prices reflect what it costs to make something properly. We'll tell you exactly what that is — materials, labour, margin — because you deserve to know.
04
Repair, Don't Replace
Every VÖRN piece comes with a lifetime repair guarantee. We'd rather fix a coat than sell you another one. That's not charity — it's how good things should work.
05
Zero Waste
Fabric offcuts go to artisan schools across India. Dead-stock materials are reimagined into limited archive pieces. Nothing leaves our atelier without purpose.
06
Long Game
We're not building a brand for an exit. We're building a house — the kind you pass down, the kind that means something, the kind worth building slowly.

Our Journey

THE Story

2018
VÖRN is founded in a London studio
First collection of 12 pieces. Sold out in 3 weeks through word of mouth alone.
2019
Partnership with Napoli tailor house
Marco Lentini joins as Head Tailor, bringing 28 years of experience in Neapolitan tailoring.
2020
Lifetime repair guarantee launched
An industry first. Every piece sold before this date was retroactively included.
2022
Japan sourcing relationship established
Reiko Suzuki joins to manage our Kojima denim and Tokyo fabric partnerships.
2024
India flagship opens in Mumbai
Our first permanent retail space. A quiet room for quiet clothes in Bandra.
2025

120 pieces. 12 artisans. One single-minded obsession with getting it right.
