Our Story

NOIR was founded in 2019 with a singular obsession: to bring the finest luxury fashion to those who refuse to compromise. We are curators, not just retailers — every piece in our collection is chosen for its artistry, its craft, and the feeling it evokes.

From the ateliers of Milan to the workshops of Jaipur, we source from artisans who share our reverence for the extraordinary. Fashion, to us, is not just clothing — it is the language of identity.

Today, NOIR serves over 80,000 discerning clients across India and beyond, with a curation of 370+ luxury pieces that evolve with every season, every mood.

Our Philosophy

We believe in slow fashion — not in pace, but in intention. Every item we carry is designed to be worn across seasons, across decades. We resist the disposable. We celebrate the enduring.

Sustainability is not our badge — it is our baseline. We work exclusively with partners who pay fair wages, use ethical materials, and design for longevity. Because real luxury doesn't come at the cost of the world.

At NOIR, you're not buying a product. You're investing in a piece of the world — one that carries history, artistry, and a certain kind of magic.
