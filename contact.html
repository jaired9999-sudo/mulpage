Common Questions

FAQ

What is your returns policy?
We offer 30-day free returns for all unworn items in original packaging. Simply contact us and we'll arrange collection at no charge.
Do you offer alterations?
Yes — free alterations are included on all core collection pieces. Bring your garment to our Mumbai store or ship it to us with your alteration request.
How long does delivery take?
Standard delivery is 3–5 business days within India. Express delivery (next day in Mumbai and Delhi) available for ₹599. International delivery 5–10 days.
Can I order a custom piece?
Yes, we accept custom commission requests for orders of 2 or more pieces. Lead time is typically 8–10 weeks. Contact us at alterations@vorn.co to begin.
What sizes do you stock?
We stock XS through XXL in most pieces. Trouser waists from 28 to 38. We also offer made-to-measure on select styles — ask in store or by email.
How do I care for my VÖRN pieces?
Care instructions vary by garment. Wool and cashmere: dry clean or cold hand-wash. Linen and cotton: machine wash cold, hang to dry. See each product page for specifics.
